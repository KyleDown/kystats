Stat Explanations with calculators

KyStat Explanation

1. MT MeaningfulTouch:

Meaningful Touch is a category that sums up all the (FG+3P+FT+TRB+AST+STL+BLK) that are registered.

Times that team did something to the ball that affected the game in a positive way.

3P is counted for in FG but I think it's important to give it credit again since 3>2.

EX: (FG=47, 3P=0, FT=19, TRB=44, AST=33, STL= 8, BLK= 6) MT= 157

--------

2. PP PlayersPlayed:

Simply how many players played? This stat is more a side kick stat.

Notice if a team's lineup is becoming more tight.

Regular season will probably play more players than they do in the playoffs. Pre pre-season usually plays more players.

EX: Bulls - SP, MJ, HG, JP, BC, WP, BJA, SW, BH, CH, CL PP=11

--------

3. YE YearsEx:

This calculates all the years of experience the players have.

Shows how experienced teams are compared to their opponent.

EX: The Bulls in 1992 ECF1 have a YE=66 while there Opp the heat have YE= 27

--------

4. TP TeamsPlayed

What teams in the NBA/ABA are being represented

Teams are only counted once, so the max is 30. So this could be represented as a percentage as well.

The data to get this value could be saved and see if any team shows up more than others.

Ex: Bulls 1992 - 1.Bulls, 2.Spurs, 3.Jazz, 4.Kings, 5.Bucks, 6.Suns, 7.Clippers, 8.Pistons, 9.Hawks - TP=9

--------

5. TAS TotalAllStar

This will show how much star power a team has.

Total number of all star appearances. Some say this could influence referee descion making.

EX: Bulls 1992 - MJ=7 and SP=4 - TAS=11

--------

6. StartsStarting

How many starts does the starting lineup have?

This stat starts at the beginning.

This shows how much chemistry a team has together.

EX: Bulls 1992 - (MJ=82, SP=80, HG=79, JP=8, BC=3 +another 5 for that game) SS=330

--------

7. PAP PossibleAcumulatedPoints

This calculates all the attempts together to get a possible accumulated point total.
